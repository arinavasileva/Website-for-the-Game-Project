ALIEN WEATHER

You are an alien who has traveled to Earth to study the weather conditions on this yet researched planet.
Unfortunately, Your ship has crashed, but you still must complete your mission!
Now instead of your ship, you must travel as earthlings do: by plane!
Flying from Airport to airport, you will observe and record strange new weather phenomena.
And since you posses superior intelligence and knowledge of the disastrous effects of global warming, you have a limited CO2 budget.
Good luck, Earth Explorer!

Easy Tutotial How to Play Our Game


The purpose of the game is to reach 5 of the 8 goals from the goal table of the flight_game database. Players begin in Helsinki Vantaa airport with a CO2 budget of 10000 credits. Each "flight" will deduct CO2 from the player's budget based on the number of kilometers travelled. Players can fly to any airport in the world as long as they have enough budgeted CO2 for the trip.

Step 1

First, enter your username, so it can be saved in the database.

Step 2

Then you will see a list of options and make your first move: check your current location, view goals, check your CO2 budget, travel to a new airport.

Step 3

When you achieve five out of eight weather goals, you win the game.
